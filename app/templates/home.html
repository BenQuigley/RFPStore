About

Question Answer
What does this site do? This site is called the "RFP Factory." You upload a question-and-answer sheet in CSV format to it, and it will format the contents for you as a stylish Bootstrap web page, an RFP Store.
What is the RFP Store for? It's an knowledge base: an easy-to-maintain and easy-to-use reference guide for your coworkers, family, or clients.
What do I do with the RFP Store page? Your RFP Store page will be a static web page, so you can host this static page online if you like, or just keep it in your private Dropbox or SharePoint instance if you prefer to keep your knowledge base private.
What happens when I need to edit my RFP Store? You could always edit the raw HTML, but the easiest way is just to keep your original CSV source file around. When a new question and answer need to be added to the knowledge base, just add it to the spreadsheet, and upload it here again to create a new RFP Store.
How does it work? You can view the source code by clicking the "Source Code" link at the bottom of the page.
